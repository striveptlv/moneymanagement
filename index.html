Money Management Dashboard

Daily Money Management Practice

This program gives patients and clients a safe way to practice financial management, app navigation, computer use, typing, multi-tasking, divided attention, and managing distraction while completing realistic online tasks. These activities support improved independence and safety with IADLs, especially for people recovering after a stroke or brain injury.

Choose one of the practice environments below to work on logging in, locating account details, switching attention between screens, following task steps, and completing functional payment or banking activities.
